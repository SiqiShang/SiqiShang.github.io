尚思齐 Siqi Shang

I received my Master’s degree in computer science from Columbia University, where I worked in Robotic Manipulation and Mobility (ROAM) Lab, advised by Professor Matei Ciocarlie. Before joining Columbia, I received a Bachelor’s degree in computer science at CUHK, where I worked closely with Professor Darwin Lau and Professor Zili Shao. My research interests are in machine learning for robotics.
